How to

Instruction on how to flash Phantom Kernel in your device.

Required custom recovery (TWRP, CWM, Philz) to be installed.



  • Follow the steps to flash the Kernel.
  • 1. Boot into the latest custom recovery
  • 2. Dirty Flash the stock ROM
  • 3. Flash the Kernel zip file
  • 4. Wipe Cache & Dalvik Cache (Optional)
  • 5. Reboot to the System
  • 6. Enjoy the Kernel
